The Brand

Where street culture
meets sculpture.

THE GRAMPS is born at the intersection of urban art, collectible culture, and fine craftsmanship. Each figure is a statement — a limited piece that bridges the gap between the street and the gallery.

Inspired by the raw energy of city walls and the quiet permanence of sculpture, every drop is a conversation between tradition and rebellion.
